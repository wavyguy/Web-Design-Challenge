Wind Speed and Latitude






In the Northern Hemisphere, as distance from the equator increases, so does wind speed. Most of the cities in our project had wind speeds less than 20 mph. The variance of wind speed for each city also increases with distance away from the equator. Because the wind speed of most cities is less than 20 mph at significant distances from the equator, the increased variance could be the cause of the relationship between wind speed and latitude.
