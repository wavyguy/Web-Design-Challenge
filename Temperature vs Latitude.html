Max Temperature and Latitude






From this graph, we see that the closer an area is to 0 degrees latitude, the higher the temperature. More interestingly, however, is the fact that the southern hemisphere tends to be warmer this time of year than the northern hemisphere. This may be due to the tilt of the earth.
